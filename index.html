Who I am

I'm Ishbarna Kafle, a penetration tester at YetiCyberOps Pvt. Ltd., based in Nepal. I focus on identifying and exploiting vulnerabilities in web applications, with an emphasis on practical, real-world impact.

I am a Computer Engineering student passionate about understanding real-world security flaws and improving my skills through hands-on practice and lab environments.My work spans web application penetration testing, security assessments, and finding vulnerability across different platforms.

When I'm not hunting bugs, I participate in CTF challenges, attend cybersecurity workshops, and continuously learn about new vulnerabilities and attack patterns. I also contribute to and engage with the college and local cybersecurity community in Nepal.
